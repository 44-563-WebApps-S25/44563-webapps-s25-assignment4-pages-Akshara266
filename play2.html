Merry Wives of Windsor

Act 1, Scene 2

Windsor. Before PAGE's house

Enter PAGE, FENTON, and ROBIN

PAGE

I will not have the doctor’s work done by you, my boy. What’s the matter?

FENTON

I have no answer. I am in a hurry.

PAGE

I shall know more of you before the hour’s done. But here’s a gentleman who can tell us all.

Enter SIR HUGH EVANS

SIR HUGH EVANS

The gentlewoman is a good woman, master Page, and deserves well of you and your family.
